COMPANY OVERVIEW

NALIFT is a leading manufacturer of articulated forklifts and warehouse equipment, dedicated to providing innovative solutions for efficient material handling. Founded in 2005, we have grown to become one of the most trusted names in the industry, with a global presence and a commitment to excellence.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and rigorous quality control processes ensure that every product we deliver meets the highest standards of performance, reliability, and safety. We invest heavily in research and development to stay at the forefront of technological innovation and to continuously improve our products.

At NALIFT, we understand that every warehouse operation is unique, which is why we work closely with our customers to provide customized solutions that address their specific needs and challenges. Our team of experienced engineers and industry experts is dedicated to helping our customers optimize their warehouse operations and achieve maximum efficiency.

OUR MISSION

To provide innovative and efficient material handling solutions that help our customers optimize their warehouse operations and achieve maximum productivity.

OUR VISION

To be the global leader in articulated forklift technology and to set the standard for innovation, quality, and customer service in the material handling industry.

OUR HISTORY

2005

Foundation

NALIFT was founded with a vision to revolutionize warehouse operations through innovative material handling solutions.

2008

First Articulated Forklift

Launch of our first articulated forklift, setting new standards for narrow aisle operations.

2012

International Expansion

Expansion into international markets with the establishment of distribution networks across Asia, Europe, and North America.

2015

Innovation Center

Opening of our state-of-the-art Research and Development Center, dedicated to driving innovation in material handling technology.

2018

Advanced Manufacturing

Implementation of Industry 4.0 technologies in our manufacturing processes, enhancing efficiency and quality control.

2020

Sustainable Initiative

Launch of our eco-friendly product line, focusing on energy efficiency and reduced environmental impact.

Today

Global Leader

NALIFT continues to lead the industry with innovative solutions, serving customers in over 50 countries worldwide.
